# MEDIA.md — GOOP profile

## Purpose

Develop a media/video pipeline through Senter's evidence-driven process.

## Context

- live-video editing
- footage slicing
- generated B-roll
- useful graphs
- overlays and general edits
- independent animated videos
- article and infographic illustration

## Evolution

Repeated operations should become Skills. Coherent media skill clusters may become Agents. Stable media workflows may become proposed Loops.

## Safety

Preserve source footage. Use reversible edits. Require approval before publishing or deleting source material.

## Lifecycle

Collect → Generate → Evaluate → Apply.

Application remains proposal-only until explicitly approved.
